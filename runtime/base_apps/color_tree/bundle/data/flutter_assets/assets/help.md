# Color Tree Help

## What is Color Tree?

Color Tree is a musical scale exploration tool. It allows you to create and explore scales by choosing:

- **Row**: The number of notes in your scale (1-11)
- **Brightness**: How the scale extends from the root (darker = more fourths, brighter = more fifths)

## Circle of Fifths

The circle shows all 12 notes arranged by perfect fifths. Click any note to adjust your scale. The highlighted arc shows your current scale selection.

## Color Legend

- **Gold** - Root Note: The foundation of your scale
- **Cyan** - In Scale: Notes that are part of your current scale
- **Grey** - Out of Scale: Notes not in your current scale

## Modes

### Highlight

Highlights your current scale on your chosen layout.

### Rows

Creates a layout where each row plays the scale at different octaves. Best with 8 or fewer notes.

## Brightness Explained

Brightness controls the balance of your scale around the root:

- **0 (Dark)**: All notes are counterclockwise from root (fourths)
- **Max (Bright)**: All notes are clockwise from root (fifths)

Try exploring the different brightness levels to see how they affect sound.
